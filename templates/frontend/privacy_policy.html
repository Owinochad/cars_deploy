Privacy Policy

At MyDreamCarAfrica, operated by Polepole Cash Ltd, we are fully committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of our customers and site visitors across Kenya and Africa. We understand the trust you place in us when providing your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our privacy commitment to you. Rest assured, we do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing their products or services without your consent. If you have any questions about how we protect your privacy, please contact us at info@mydreamcar.africa.

MydreamCarAfrica is a lottery competition platform owned and operated by Polepole Cash Ltd, a company registered in Kenya. Any reference in this policy to “MydreamCarAfrica,” “we,” “us,” or “our” is to Polepole Cash Ltd.

When you interact with us through our website (or other means), we may collect certain personal information from which you can be identified (referred to as personal data). For the purposes of the Kenyan Data Protection Act and other applicable laws, we are the "data controller," meaning that we are responsible for determining how your personal data is used and ensuring its protection.

We are committed to ensuring your privacy and will take all necessary steps to comply with legal obligations when handling your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we meet this commitment, so please review it carefully.

WHAT THIS POLICY COVERS:

  1. The types of personal data you provide or we collect when using our website or interacting with us;
  2. How and why we use your data, and the legal reasons for doing so;
  3. Who we share your data with;
  4. Your rights over your data and how you can exercise those rights; and
  5. How to contact us with any questions or concerns.

WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT, AND HOW DO WE USE IT?

When you register with MyDreamCarAfrica or interact with us, you may provide the following types of personal information:

Types of Information Collected:

  • Identity Information: First name, surname, login information, country of residence
  • Contact Information: Email address, telephone numbers, and physical address
  • Financial Information: Payment card details, billing address, purchase history, and payment history
  • Profile Information: Marketing preferences, website preferences, feedback through reviews and surveys
  • Social Media Information: If you register via Facebook, Twitter, or Google, we will use your social media contact details to populate your MyDreamCarAfrica registration page.

We may also collect the following information using cookies or other tracking technologies:

Usage Information:

  • Website usage data, such as time spent on pages, click-throughs, and download errors
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device details, operating system, and configuration

WHO DO WE SHARE YOUR DATA WITH?

We do not share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes without your consent. However, we may share data with trusted third-party providers who help us deliver our services, including:

  • Processing payments and hosting services
  • Running our competitions (auditors, judges, professional advisors)

We ensure that these third parties handle your data confidentially and securely. Additionally, we may share your data if required by law or regulation.

DATA SECURITY:

We adopt industry-standard security measures to protect your data, such as encrypted connections (SSL) and secure servers. While we take all reasonable steps to safeguard your data, please note that online data transmission is not 100% secure. Any transmission is at your own risk.

DATA RETENTION:

We will retain your data for as long as you hold a MyDreamCarAfrica account or where required for legal or tax purposes. When no longer needed, your data will be securely deleted.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond to all legitimate requests within one month.
