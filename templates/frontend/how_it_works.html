Charity

FAQs

How do I enter the competition?

To enter:

  1. Visit the website and select the competition you wish to enter.
  2. Choose the required number of entries and proceed to checkout.
  3. Log in or register if not already logged in.
  4. Agree to the Terms and Conditions and complete the purchase.

You will receive an email confirming your entry.

Where is my email confirming my purchase and ticket numbers?

Your tickets will be displayed on your profile page or you will receive a confirmation email with your ticket numbers within 10-15 minutes. During high demand, this may take a few hours. If you're looking for your ticket number, refer to the next FAQ.

Where can I see my ticket numbers?

Once logged in, select ‘My Profile’ from your account dropdown in the top-right corner. Here, you will find all your ticket numbers for the competitions you have entered. Good luck!

How will I know if I have won?

We will notify the winner via telephone or email within 7 days of the competition closing date. If your contact details change before then, please inform us. If we cannot reach you within 14 days, we reserve the right to select another winner.

How do I know who has won?

You can view past winners on the ‘Previous Winners’ page. Some winners have kindly shared photos as well!

How do I know if a draw is live?

All major prizes are drawn live with a presenter. You can watch these on our Draws page, Facebook, and YouTube, where we randomly generate the winner.

How long is the competition open for?

The competition’s opening and closing dates are listed on the website. We do not extend closing dates, but live draw dates may be brought forward if the competition sells out early.

Can anyone enter the competition?

Our competitions are open to residents over 18 in the countries we operate in. Employees, agents, suppliers, or their immediate family members cannot enter.

What are the prizes?

Prizes are described on the website. We reserve the right to offer an alternative prize of equal or higher value if necessary.

Is there a cash alternative?

If a cash alternative is available, it will be clearly stated in the competition details. For tech competitions, cash alternatives are more common. Feel free to ask us!

How do you use my personal data?

We use your data to administer the competition and award prizes. We do not share it with third parties except where necessary. For full details, refer to our Privacy Policy.

If I win, do I have to participate in promotional exercises?

No, this is not compulsory. However, with your permission, we would love to share your excitement on our website and social media.

How is the winner decided?

Once the competition closes, the full entry list is posted on the website. We draw the winning number live or use PHP’s random_int for automated draws.

What are my chances of winning?

Your chances depend on the number of entries. For example, in a competition with 3,000 entries, buying 1 entry gives you a 1 in 3,000 chance of winning. Buying more entries increases your odds.

What if a competition does not sell out?

The draw will still go ahead on the scheduled date. You may get better odds if fewer entries are sold. We do not change the prize or base it on ticket sales.

Can I get a refund of my entry fee?

We do not offer refunds for entry fees.

Why is my bank showing payments as a 'subscription'?

We do not offer subscription services. It’s likely your bank’s system is labeling the payment this way, but rest assured there is no recurring payment. Cancelling with your bank may block future entries.

What checks do cars for competitions go through?

All cars undergo checks, including a PDI, fresh 12-month MOT (if needed), and necessary servicing by reputable specialists or main dealers.
